Chatmola|pack of 20

Chatmola is a confectionery product created by Anil Food Industries, which was founded in 1972 by Ramesh Gera. Chatmola is a chewy digestive tablet that's made with dried mango powder, black pepper, salts, and other natural spices. It's prepared and packed in hygienic conditions to ensure quality and flavor. Chatmola is available in a sweet and sour flavor called Chatpati Goli
